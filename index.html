ABOUT ME

WHO AM I

My name is Maksym Vlasenko, I come from the picturesque Sumy region of Ukraine, which was under occupation. Having been born and raised in this country, I feel immense pride in my roots and the rich cultural heritage that surrounds me.

My father, a devoted man, took up the noble cause of serving in the Ukrainian army. This inspired me to do my part, so I decided to hold a fundraiser to purchase necessary military supplies.

In an effort to support my father and his comrades, I diligently seek funds to purchase the essentials that will increase their efficiency and well-being. It's a small but meaningful gesture to ensure they have the tools they need to do their vital work. Thank you everyone for your help and support
